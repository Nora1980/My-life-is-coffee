Americano

An Americano is a coffee drink made by adding hot water to espresso. It's a simple preparation, often resembling a drip coffee in appearance, but with the distinct flavor profile of espresso. While the name suggests it's a simple dilution of espresso, there's some nuance in how it's prepared and the resulting taste.

Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee produced by forcing hot water under high pressure through finely ground coffee beans. Originating in Italy, espresso has become one of the most popular coffee-brewing methods worldwide.

Doppio

Doppio is a double shot of espresso, meaning it's made with twice the amount of ground coffee and water compared to a single espresso shot. This results in a stronger, more concentrated drink with a bolder flavor and higher caffeine content.

Cortado

A cortado is a Spanish coffee drink consisting of espresso cut with a roughly equal amount of warm milk. The milk is steamed, but not foamed, to reduce the acidity of the espresso. It's known for its balanced flavor and relatively small size.

$ 28.0

Red Eye Coffee

Red Eye Coffee is a caffeinated beverage that combines a standard cup of brewed drip coffee with a shot of espresso. It's known for its high caffeine content and is a popular choice for those needing an extra boost of energy, often nicknamed "shot in the dark".

$ 25.0

Galao

Galao is a hot drink from Portugal made by adding foamed milk to espresso coffee. Similar to caffe latte or cafe au lait, it consists of about one quarter coffee and three quarters foamed milk.

$ 26.0

Lungo

A lungo is an Italian coffee drink made by using an espresso machine to pull a longer shot of espresso, using more water than a traditional espresso. It results in a larger,milder, and more diluted coffee compared to a regular espresso, with a longer extraction time.

$ 28.0

Macchiato

A macchiato is a coffee beverage made with espresso and a small amount of milk, usually foamed, and the "macchiato" means "stained" or "marked" in Italian. It's essentially espresso "stained" with a small amount of milk.

$ 30.0

Irish

Affogato

Affogato coffee, dessert and your after dinner drink, all rolled into one. This is what you have when you're "too full for dessert"! Just pour espresso coffee over vanilla gelato or ice cream, enjoy the melty swirls and dig in. The Italians are genius.

Flat White

A flat white is a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk, known for its smooth, velvety texture and a thin layer of microfoam. It generally has a higher proportion of espresso to milk than a latte, and less foam than a cappuccino.

$ 30.0

Ristretto

A ristretto is a short shot of espresso made with the same amount of ground coffee as a standard espresso but exxtracted with half the amount of water. This results in a more concentrated, intense, and sweeter flavor profile compared to a standard espresso. The term "restretto" is Italian for "restricted," reflecting the limited amount of water used in its preparation.

$ 28.0
